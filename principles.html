    The Silver Creek Standard

    At Silver Creek, we’re more than just a supplier — we’re a partner in performance. Our mission is to lead the energy supply industry through advanced technology, deep product knowledge, and a Midwest-rooted work ethic. From the ground up, our team is committed to delivering smarter, faster, and more reliable solutions.

    Founded in Kansas and built on decades of experience, Silver Creek continues to grow while remaining focused on quality, trust, and innovation in every relationship we build.

  • 02.

    Built from the Ground Up

    Our story began in 1979 in Wichita, KS — and since then, Silver Creek has grown into a trusted name across the Midwest and beyond. What started with a single location has expanded into a robust network of distribution centers and service hubs, each driven by the same founding principles.

    We’re proud partners with leading manufacturers like Ameriforge, Apollo Valve, Anvil, and more — ensuring our clients always get top-tier equipment backed by proven performance.

  • 03.

    Rooted in Integrity

    Everything we do at Silver Creek is grounded in honesty, humility, and hard work. Our relationships — whether with customers, suppliers, or team members — are built on trust and transparency. It's not just how we operate — it's who we are.

    We don’t believe in cutting corners. We believe in showing up, being accountable, and delivering exactly what we say we will — every time.

    Committed to Quality

    Quality isn’t a buzzword — it’s our baseline. With 24/7 support from Silver Creek employees, in-house pump repair, compressor service, and mobile parts trailers for wellsite completions, we’re equipped to handle it all. We deliver excellence — not excuses.
